TasikCode is a vibrant community of open code enthusiasts based in Tasikmalaya, dedicated to fostering collaboration, innovation, and learning in the world of technology. 

We bring together developers, designers, and tech lovers to share knowledge, explore new technologies, and work on exciting projects. Through workshops, hackathons, and meetups, TasikCode empowers members to enhance their skills, build a strong network, and contribute to open-source initiatives, creating a supportive environment for both beginners and experienced professionals. 

Join us to connect, learn, and shape the future of technology in our region!
